Jamie O'Neill, Pharm.D.

Bio

Boredom is the enemy of innovation. I find it difficult to settle in to a monotonous cycle and love exploring new ideas which explains the zig-zagging pattern of my life. Growing up I focused more on creativity than academics and started college as a fashion design major.

I fell in love with Philosophy & Science and received a Doctorate in Pharmacy from the University of Georgia in 2008. After several years of working as a retail pharmacist, I decided that I wanted to try to do more to give back to society so I became an entrepreneur pioneering in a new sector of the pharmacy industry - personalized medication management services for seniors.

This endevour taught me a lot about politics and barriers to innovation in the healthcare industry. I closed my business down in July of 2019 and started a new chapter in my life learning how to be a computer programmer. I am very excited to see where this path leads me!

My heart is full with love to and from my best friend, Patrick, and our 7lb. fur baby, Juan Pablo. My favorite thing to do is go on adventures around the globe. I also enjoy attending music festivals with friends and family. I have diverse taste in music and art and a passion for philosophy and theology.
